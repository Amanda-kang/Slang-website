About

Slangs are a part of our daily life conversations and have been integrated into different contexts of our life. These expressions can be hilarious, some may be a little offensive and some can be quite interesting to look at.

They are defined as a type of language consisting of words and phrases that are regarded as very informal, are more common in speech than writing, and are typically restricted to a particular context or group of people.

But how much do we know about them?
